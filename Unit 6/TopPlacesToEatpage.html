    Freds

    Fred's is a resturant that only serves breakfeast. Its a great way to kick off you day.
    Its a little shack that serves about fifty at a time, and makes about any breakfeast food you would want.
    Some popular foods on their menu are french toast, scrambled eggs, and personally my favorite, cinamon buns.
    Although Freds serves fantatstic food, it's not easy to get to. The parking is very limited and to actually get into
    the restrurant you need to be a member or have a guest card, without a card you cannot get in.
    But once you get in, it's so worth it and I highly recommed you check it out at you vist to New Hope.

    Spatola's Pizza

    Spatola's Pizza is a very popular eating spot in town because it's the only pizza place. It serves a wide variety of pizzas like,
    pasta pizza and cheese steak pizza(with or without sauce). In addition , Spatola's offers free refills on any kind of drink you ordered.
    This resturant will not take a long time to eat and is a good pitstop on your trip to New Hope. After about fifteen minutes
    eating you should be on your way again!

    Moo Burger

    Moo Burger is a cute little resturant that offers healthy and great tasting burgers. It was created three years ago
    but despite it's high prices, it still generates a lot of interest in their burgurs. But not only do you get a burger,
    you get fries and a milkshake if you so choose. Now you may be thinking, this sounds like McDonals,
    well no these burgers are much more healthier than the ones McDonalds make, and taste a whole lot better as well.
    Just like Spatola's this is a quicker way to eat as it would be a good stop on your journey through New Hope.
